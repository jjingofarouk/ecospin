Our Solution

Transforming plastic waste into high-quality recycled yarn and artificial wool while empowering local communities.

How We Make a Difference

EcoSpin is a community-driven initiative that provides practical, small-scale recycling solutions to tackle plastic waste in Uganda. We work with local youth, waste collectors, and small businesses to turn discarded plastic into valuable textile materials.

Our Process

1. Waste Collection

We collaborate with local waste collectors, youth groups, and informal recyclers to collect plastic waste, mainly PET bottles, from households, markets, and streets.

2. Community Sorting & Cleaning

Plastic is sorted and cleaned using affordable, locally built machinery made by youth engineers from Katwe. This ensures low-cost and sustainable operations.

3. Shredding & Processing

Using low-energy shredders, we process plastics into flakes, which are then melted and spun into synthetic fibers. This technique allows us to produce recycled yarn and artificial wool for local textile and craft industries.

4. Crafting & Textile Production

We supply recycled yarn to tailors, knitters, and artisans, helping them create sustainable products. This supports local businesses while reducing environmental waste.

Why It Matters

Reducing Plastic Pollution

Each kilogram of plastic recycled prevents pollution in water bodies and landfills, directly improving Uganda’s environment.

Creating Jobs & Opportunities

By training local youth and women in waste management and textile production, EcoSpin fosters sustainable employment.

Empowering Small Businesses

We provide affordable, locally made recycled fibers to small-scale tailors and artisans, helping them grow their businesses sustainably.
